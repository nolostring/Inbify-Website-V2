Why Inbify

There’s a lot of ways to collect feedbacks, but there’s a lot of friction on them too.

  • Send pitch info
  • Send actual methods and why are they obsolete
  • Send brief copy description of why our service and not the others (en sol pitch hay algo de copy)

Inbify VS actual methods

Inbify:

  • SMS has a 90% of response rate
  • Real-time communication with customer
  • SMS is the most used communication channel

Net Promoter Score:

  • 10% response rate from customers
  • The business needs the customer email to get his feedback
  • Don’t provide insights on how things can get fixed

Survey Forms:

  • Takes to much time to fill
  • 80% of customers abandon surveys halfway through
  • Customers engagement have drop to from 20% to 2% in the last years

Online Reviews:

  • One bad review takes 12 positive ones to make it up
  • Just 15 out of 1,000 customers are writes online reviews
  • Customers need to create an account to review businesses

Suggestion card:

  • Just 5% of customers fill them
  • Analyze the cards takes to much time for the manager
  • Much of the suggestions never gets to the owner
